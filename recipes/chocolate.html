Dark Dark Chocolate

Oh you haven't seen dark chocolate like this before

DO NOT IN FACT MAKE THIS!! THE INGREDIENTS ARE MODIFIED BY WEIRDDDUDE FOR FUN, YOU WILL GET SICK

Your Demise

  • 2 cups coconut oil
  • 8 tablespoons honey or more for taste
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • pinch of sea salt
  • 5 cups unprocessed 100% cocoa powder or cacao powder

The Ending

  1. In a small pot over a low heat: melt the coconut oil.
  2. Stir in the honey, vanilla (or your preferred flavourings) and salt.
  3. Remove the mixture from the heat. Add the cocoa powder and mix until it's dissolved into the chocolate mixture. You should have a hellishly lumpy chocolate sauce.
  4. Pour the chocolate into silicone moulds or a lined baking pan and transfer to the freezer for at least 3 hours or in the fridge for at least 5 hours. Once they've hardened pop them out of the moulds and enjoy.
